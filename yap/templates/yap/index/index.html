How to Join an Event




Step 1: Signup for an account, then find an event about a topic that interests you and RSVP.



Step 2: Fifteen minutes prior to the start of the event, you will receive an email with a link to the small-group video chat room. The link will also be available on the event page.



Step 3: Enjoy socializing and discussing a topic of mutual interest with others.






How to Create an Event




Step 1: Signup for an account, then create a group with a specific theme. You could discuss books, sports, languages, or whatever interests you.



Step 2: After creating a group, you can create an event for the group. You select the name of the event, as well as the date and time among other details.



Step 3: Share the event with your friends and anyone else interested. There are buttons on the event page making it convenient to share the event's URL via email, SMS, or social media.



Step 4: Fifteen minutes prior to the start of the event, the attendees receive an email with a link to the small-group video chat room. The link will also be available on the event page.



Step 5: Enjoy socializing with others.

F.A.Q.


Where do the online videochat meetups take place?

The host will provide a link to the videochat room. They may use any available platform such as Zoom, Google Meet, Jitsi Meet, Skype, etc. Jitsi Meet is free-to-use and a great choice for those who don't have a paid account on another platform.


Can anyone use this to organize online meetups?

Yes, it's free to use and registration just takes a few seconds.


Any plans for future feature additions?

Yes, I plan on integrating PayPal so users can host paid events if they want. Please email hello at yapspot dot com with any feature requests.
